About Us

INTRINO INFRATECH Pvt. Ltd. has been a supplier of multi-technical services and Electronic Manufacturing for over two decades in India. Established in 2001 formerly known as BALAJI Enterprises, INTRINO INFRATECH Pvt. Ltd. has a rich history in providing integrated solutions and its expertise to clients across various electrical, mechanical and networking fields and OEM supply. In providing these solutions, INTRINO INFRATECH works across the value chain from Manufacturing,consulting, installation, execution to maintenance in the commercial, industrial, data center and retail sectors. INTRINO INFRATECH has successfully completed over 100 projects across multiple industries in India. We undertake customized turnkey projects in Commercial, Data Centers, Industrial and warehouses for a diverse range of reputed clientele.INTRINO INFRATECH is a multi-state licensed contractor
